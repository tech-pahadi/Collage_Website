Courses We Offer

RJIT provides a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and computer applications
designed to meet modern industry needs and support innovation.

Computer Science & Engineering

Our Computer Science & Engineering program focuses on developing strong programming, software development, and problem-solving skills. Students gain expertise in areas like artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity to meet the demands of the tech industry.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Electronics & Communication Engineering course trains students in designing and maintaining electronic devices and communication systems. It covers modern technologies like IoT, wireless networks, and embedded systems to prepare students for innovation-driven careers.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering at RJIT emphasizes core concepts of mechanics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing. Students learn to design, analyze, and improve mechanical systems, equipping them for roles in automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries.
